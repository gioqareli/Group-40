Simple Omlette Recipe


An easy and qiuck dish, perfect for any meal.This clasic omlette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionaly filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables or meat


Preparation time

  • Total: approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutea
  • cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional fillings:cheese,diced vegatables,cooked meats,herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs
  2. Heat the pan
  3. Cook the omlette
  4. Add the fillings (optional)
  5. Fold and serve
  6. Enjoy
